Last Friday, we hosted a hands-on workshop on data visualization using R and
ggplot2. The session covered the grammar of graphics, common plot types, and
tips for creating publication-ready figures.

## Key Takeaways

- Start with a clear question before choosing a visualization
- Use color intentionally and ensure accessibility
- Prefer direct labeling over legends when possible
- ggplot2 themes make it easy to maintain consistent styling

The workshop was led by one of our board members and attended by students from
a range of departments. We had great discussions about best practices for
presenting data in research papers and presentations.
