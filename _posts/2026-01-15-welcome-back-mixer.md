We kicked off the spring semester with our Welcome Back Mixer! Over 40 students
and faculty came together for an evening of networking, food, and fun.

It was great to see returning members reconnect and new faces join the
community. We shared our plans for the semester, including upcoming workshops,
a guest speaker series, and our annual hackathon.

Thank you to everyone who attended. If you missed it, don't worry -- we have
plenty of events coming up this semester. Check our [events page](/events/) to
stay informed.
